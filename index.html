Why Our Practice Regimen?

Tapa's practice regimen is a structured self-learning path that takes you step-by-step from A to Z of answering product design questions. It trains you on all the things you are evaluated for in the interview.

The concept-wise practice drills and feedback loops help you evaluate your own answers, know where you stand, and gives you actionable steps on how to improve yourself. We help you achieve mastery in the fastest and most painless way.
